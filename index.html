Website style guide

ABOUT COLORS I want to reference the color of the ceramic pieces which are black, terracotta, cobalt blue, off-white and a generic «mixed» color for glazed pieces. The website’s content is mostly photos so I will keep the layout clean, with white and off-white background with black typography. Kind of archival look. I don’t know yet how the colors will be used.

<h1>: Outfit regular (300)

<h2>: Outfit regular (400)

ABOUT FONTS I chose Outfit extra bold for titling and Outfit regular for text. What I like about the font is how geometric it is. The « O » are perfect circles, the « T » crosses. It feels simple and playful. I mostly work with geometric shapes in my design so I think it’s a good fit. What I also like are the ligatures which is also something I do in my work when I combine shapes in a collage. I also chose a monotype : Roboto, that I would chose for technical description and tags. I think they compliment each others nicely in their contrats.

My other option for titling and text were : Rubik, Work sans, Space grotesk and Unbounded. Other options for monotypes were IBM Plex mono and Inconsolata.
